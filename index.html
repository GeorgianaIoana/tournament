About the Tournament

A Celebration of
Strategic Excellence

The Nordic Chess Open embodies the Scandinavian philosophy of thoughtful design and purposeful competition. Since 2018, we've created a space where the world's finest chess minds gather to compete, learn, and push the boundaries of the game.

Set in Stockholm's historic cultural quarter, our tournament combines world-class competition with the calm, focused atmosphere that defines Nordic culture. Every detail is crafted to help players perform at their peak.
